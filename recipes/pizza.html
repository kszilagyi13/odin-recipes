Sausage Pizza Recipe

Description

Have you ever wanted to make pizzeria style pizza at home? If you have then you have come to the right place. This recipe will cover everything you need to make a delcious sausage pizza. We will go through the process step by step to ensure that you will be able to make a great pizza from the comfort or your own home!

Ingredients

  • Dough Ball
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Pre Heat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Shape dough into pizza
  3. Distribute sauce evenly on the dough
  4. Spread cheese over sauce
  5. Spread sausage over cheese
  6. Place pizza in oven and bake 10-12 minutes
  7. Remove from oven when crust is golden, let sit 5 minutes before cutting
  8. Enjoy your pizza!
